Top 3 places of interest in India

  • Taj Mahal

    • Symbol of India worldwide
    • UNESCO World Heritage Site
    • One of the New Seven Wonders of the World
    • Most internationally famous monument in India

  • Jammu & Kashmir

    • Srinagar, Gulmarg, Sonamarg, Dal Lake
    • Often called “Paradise on Earth”
    • Snow mountains, lakes, valleys, houseboats

  • Goa

    • India's most famous beach destination
    • Nightlife, Portuguese architecture, beaches, cafes
    • Huge domestic + international tourism
